Problem Statement

Remote agricultural regions face severe logistical challenges. Our high‑capacity drone bridges this gap by transporting loads swiftly and safely when roads or rail are inaccessible.

Market & Competition

Competition Analysis

Main competitor: E‑Hang (China)

Unlike passenger‑drone makers, we focus exclusively on logistics, reducing safety risks and accelerating regulatory approval. As an Indian company, we adhere to stringent IIT‑backed testing before deployment.

Market Size

The global commercial drone market is rapidly expanding, with Asia projected to outpace North America by year‑end. India, having legalized drones in 2018, is on track to become the world’s 3rd largest commercial market by 2024.

Service is the largest segment, while software remains the fastest growing. North America alone sold $730 M worth of non‑military drones in 2020.
