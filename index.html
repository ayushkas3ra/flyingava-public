About Us

Flying AVA Tech Pvt. Ltd. is a start-up company established in association with professors and consultants from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. Various current professors and academicians from all IITs are associated with the company.

Dr. Kamal Poddar, Professor of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, is the mentor of the company. The company primarily works to carry on the business of unmanned aerial systems, which might be autonomous or human-operated control systems. The company also provides consultation on developing products from research in the fields of Aerodynamics, Robotics (Mechanical & Electronics), Image Processing, and Electro-optics Products for industries and academics.
