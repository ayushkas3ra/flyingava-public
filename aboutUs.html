About FlyingAVA

FLYINGAVA TECH PVT. LTD IS A STARTUP WHICH PLANS TO DEVELOP NEW PRODUCTS FOR USE OF HEAVY TRANSPORT INDUSTRIES USING UAV AND ARTIFICIAL INTELIGENCE .

Our Vision

Drone Technology is already being used or planned to use in transportation of goods. Although there are still lots of possibilities left in this area. Safety ,ease of handling and mass utilization is still untouched area. A systematic transport system for handling items from place A to Place B can save lots of Time, efforts, money.
Our vision is to explore the those possibilities using artificial intelligence which can reduce time, carbon content and human effort.

Our Mission

• Our first mission is to develop a prototype product for defense industry .As we know that in border areas the supply management is biggest challenge especially at critical times so a working scaled prototype will be developed as technology demonstrator.
• After this we can pitch this prototype for various other industries like postal ,medical and ecommerce platforms.
We can pitch our product to NDRF /SDRF for there relief operations at the time of natural calamities

The Problem

As per Rising transport problems in urban and rural areas it has been a serious concern for future. Especially at border areas where road connectivity will always be a challenge and in urban areas where ecommerce has raised high impact on supply and demand.
Conventional transport systems creates lot of carbon content which has been highlighted by UNO to various heavily populated countries like India .
Chances of accidents ,late delivery ,theft are major concerns which requires technological solutions in near future.
• So in order to address above problem we need a smart solution and a design with Artificial intelligence can provide a solution.



Our Solution

FlyingAVA utilizes advanced drone technology powered by AI navigation systems to provide rapid, cost-effective, and sustainable delivery services. Our drones are equipped with GPS, sensors, and lightweight cargo bays to ensure safe and timely deliveries, regardless of the terrain.
